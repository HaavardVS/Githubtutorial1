Github tutorial



To get started with Github



Here is a tutorial that will guide you from start to finish on how to

publish and share your work with other coders around the world













Lets get started!











Step 1

Create an account



If you click here, you will be redirect you to Github´s homepage.

There you can create your own account. If you already have an account,

you can just move on to Step 2.



                                                              


Step 2

Repository




After you have made an account you need to make a

repository (bare et sted å oppbevare filene du skal dele)

then just call it something. Hit "Create Repository".

                                                              

Step 3

Upload files



Now you are ready to publish files. Tap on the button

where it says "Upload files", choose your files and

and we are ready to move on to the next step.

                                                              


Step 4

Share it to the world!



Go to settings, and scroll down to Github Pages.

Then you choose "Master Branch" and hit "save"!
